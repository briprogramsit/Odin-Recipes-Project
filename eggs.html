Scrambled Eggs and Cheese


Description

Eggs are great for your brain and getting your day started, as they are rich in protein. Total power food. I love to add fresh greens and vegetables to them to increase the pow wow!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese
  • Spinach
  • Diced tomato
  • Diced onion or scallion
  • Seasoning salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, seasoning salt, cheese, spinach, tomato and onion in bowl.
  2. Place in non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Stir eggs with forks until fluffy.
  4. ENJOY!
