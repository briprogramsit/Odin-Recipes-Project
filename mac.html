Macaroni and Cheese


Description

Who doesn't love a good bowl of macaroni and cheese, especially when it is homemade? At first, macaroni and cheese was a dish primarily served to the upper class until the Industrial Revolution made pasta production much easier. Not sure who introduced this to us, but thank you. Check out this delicious, crockpot recipe below!

Ingredients

  • Elbow noodles
  • Triple cheddar cheese
  • Almond milk
  • Parsley
  • Seasoning salt

Instructions

  1. In crockpot, pour cheese and 2 cups of almond milk.
  2. Boil noodles until al dente according to the package directions.
  3. Drain noodles and pour into cheese sauce in crockpot.
  4. Stir in seasoning salt and let cook in crockpot for 1 hour.
  5. Garnish with parsley.
  6. ENJOY!
